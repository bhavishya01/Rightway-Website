Our Journey

Rightway Textile Mills in Amritsar, with decades of experience in the textile industry, is a reputed manufacturer, supplier, and exporter of premium woolen products. Our comprehensive range includes blazer and twill fabrics, woolen blankets (including those for CAPFs), stoles, socks, caps, scarves, mufflers, mosquito nets, recycled wool blankets, shirting Angola fabrics, and sleeping bags. With a strong focus on quality, craftsmanship, and sustainability, we proudly serve both domestic and international markets.
